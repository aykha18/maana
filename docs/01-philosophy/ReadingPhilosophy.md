# ReadingPhilosophy

Reading is the primary user experience.

The product should not treat poems as short content units to skim. It should help readers
slow down, notice structure, and move from surface meaning to deeper interpretation.

## Principles

- Reading comes before interaction.
- Beauty and legibility matter.
- Commentary should clarify, not dominate.
- Navigation should support curiosity.
- Discovery should feel like guided expansion, not distraction.
