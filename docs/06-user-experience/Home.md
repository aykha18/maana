# Home

## Purpose

The home page should introduce both halves of the product:

- discover poetry
- become more ready to understand it

## Key Sections

### 1. Hero

Primary message:

"Explore humanity through poetry."

Secondary message:

"Build the capacity to understand difficult literature through questions,
experiences, symbols, and foundations."

### 2. Continue Reading

Show:

- recently viewed poems
- poem returns after foundation exploration
- unfinished reflections

### 3. Foundations to Explore

Suggested concepts or experiences:

- longing
- mortality
- irony
- transcendence

Each card should explain why exploring it will unlock richer reading.

### 4. Short Lessons

Show 3-5 lessons from the Human Experience Academy with very short durations.

### 5. Readiness Paths

Examples:

- Prepare for Ghalib
- Prepare for Dickinson
- Prepare for The Waste Land

These paths should recommend a small cluster of concepts, lessons, and poems.

### 6. Reflection Prompt

Offer a low-pressure reflective question tied to current exploration.

## UX Rules

- The home page should invite curiosity, not homework.
- The user should always be able to jump directly into reading.
- Learning paths must feel like preparation for literature, not schoolwork.
