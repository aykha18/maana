# Dust

## Definition

Dust is a symbol associated with mortality, humility, ruin, memory, travel, and return.

## Classification

Symbol

## Meaning Dimensions

- earth and materiality
- mortality
- humility
- forgottenness
- destruction
- sacred trace

## Historical Notes

Dust can indicate the fragility of human life, the remains of empire,
or the trace left by the beloved, pilgrim, saint, or exile.

## Civilization-Specific Usage

- Persian literature: often tied to humility, longing, and sacred nearness
- Urdu literature: may evoke ruin, distance, loss, and reverence
- Arabic literature: can appear in elegy, travel, devotion, and historical memory
