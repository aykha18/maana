# Beloved

## Definition

Beloved is a central figure in poetry that may represent a human lover,
a divine presence, an unattainable ideal, or the focus of longing.

## Classification

Symbolic figure

## Meaning Dimensions

- romantic object of desire
- absent or unattainable other
- divine beloved
- ideal beauty
- source of suffering and transformation

## Historical Notes

The beloved is often intentionally unstable.
The same poem may allow romantic, mystical, and philosophical readings at once.

## Civilization-Specific Usage

- Persian literature: often blurred between earthly and divine meaning
- Urdu literature: commonly layered with irony, longing, distance, and metaphysical ambiguity
- Arabic literature: may move between courtly love, longing, praise, and spiritual resonance
