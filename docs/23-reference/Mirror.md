# Mirror

## Definition

Mirror is a symbol of reflection, self-knowledge, truth, distortion, and recognition.

## Classification

Symbol

## Meaning Dimensions

- literal reflective object
- self-recognition
- spiritual reflection
- vanity
- truth and illusion

## Historical Notes

The mirror often marks the tension between appearance and reality.
In mystical reading, it may suggest purification of perception or the polishing of the self.

## Civilization-Specific Usage

- Persian literature: often associated with inner refinement and metaphysical reflection
- Urdu literature: may carry romantic, ironic, or psychological resonance
- Greek thought and literature: can suggest imitation, image, or unstable selfhood
