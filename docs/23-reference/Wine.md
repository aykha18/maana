# Wine

## Definition

Wine is a symbol with layered literary meaning across multiple traditions.

## Classification

Symbol

## Meaning Dimensions

- literal drink
- pleasure and celebration
- transgression
- mystical intoxication
- loss of control
- release from ego

## Historical Notes

Wine can appear as a social object, a poetic symbol, a mystical metaphor,
or a provocation against rigid moralism.

## Civilization-Specific Usage

- Islamic literature: often charged with tension between prohibition and metaphor
- Persian literature: frequently used in mystical and philosophical contexts
- Urdu literature: often linked with longing, irony, and poetic self-consciousness
- Arabic literature: may appear in courtly, classical, or symbolic registers
