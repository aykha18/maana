# FactChecking

Fact-checking protects the credibility of commentary, metadata, and reference content.

## Minimum Rule

No claim should appear authoritative unless the team can explain where it comes from.

## What To Verify

- poet identity and attribution
- poem title and source
- translation attribution
- historical claims
- civilization-specific symbol usage
- quotations and references

## Editorial Standard

When evidence is uncertain:

- say that it is uncertain
- separate fact from interpretation
- avoid overstating symbolic meaning

## Relation To Ontology

Fact-checking is especially important when defining:

- themes
- symbols
- concepts
- commentary claims
- reference articles

The ontology should be rigorous, but the supporting claims inside it must also be defensible.
