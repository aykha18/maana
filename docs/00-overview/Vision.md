# Vision

Build the definitive digital atlas of world poetry.

A reader should be able to move from one verse to a network of meanings: themes,
symbols, emotions, histories, and civilizations. The product should make poetry easier
to enter without making it intellectually shallow.

## Vision Statement

Maana helps people move from text to understanding and explore humanity through poetry.

## Long-Term Picture

- a reader-friendly poetry library
- a knowledge graph of literary meaning
- an editorially curated reference for students and researchers
- a bridge across languages and civilizations
