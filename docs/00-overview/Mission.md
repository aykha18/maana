# Mission

Create a reading experience that explains poetry without flattening it.

## Mission Statement

Maana exists to help readers understand classical and literary poetry
through context, commentary, symbolism, and relationships.

## Core Commitments

- Keep reading at the center of the product.
- Treat poetry as knowledge, not just content.
- Prefer human-curated understanding over shallow summaries.
- Design for cross-cultural exploration.
- Preserve nuance, ambiguity, and layered interpretation.
