# QualityChecklist

Use this checklist before marking a poem as editorially ready.

## Identity

- poem identity is complete
- poet attribution is checked
- source language is correct

## Text

- original text is verified
- verse segmentation is consistent
- source edition is known or documented

## Translation

- translation attribution is present
- translation type is clear
- key ambiguities are noted where needed

## Ontology

- human questions are assigned where relevant
- human experiences are assigned where relevant
- themes and symbols use canonical ontology terms

## Commentary

- commentary distinguishes observation from interpretation
- major claims are evidence-aware
- contradictory readings are handled honestly

## Evidence And Citations

- substantive claims have evidence classes
- citations exist for source text and translation
- citations exist for major interpretive claims where appropriate

## Editorial Status

- coverage level assigned
- quality level assigned
- review status recorded
