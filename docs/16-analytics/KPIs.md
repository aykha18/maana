# KPIs

## Product KPIs

- poem sessions per active reader
- concept and experience exploration depth
- poem return rate after prerequisite exploration

## Reader Formation KPIs

- lessons completed per active learner
- reflections saved per active learner
- percentage of readers with at least one readiness path completed
- conversion from foundation view to poem return

## Quality KPIs

- average dwell time on foundation pages
- average dwell time on poem pages after lesson completion
- cross-civilizational reading spread after academy usage

## Guardrail KPIs

- drop-off caused by overlong prerequisite flows
- percentage of poem visits blocked by unnecessary education prompts
- reflection fatigue rate
