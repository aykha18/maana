# Verses

This document defines the verse as the smallest core reading unit we model explicitly.

## Definition

A verse is a meaningful structural unit within a poem. In some traditions it may be a line,
a couplet, or another canonical subdivision.

## Why It Matters

- themes may appear in one verse but not the whole poem
- symbols may cluster in specific verses
- translations often differ verse by verse
- commentary is often attached to a small textual unit
