# HumanExperiences

Human experiences are one of the primary ways readers explore the corpus.

## Definition

A human experience is a broad lived condition, emotional state, or existential situation
that helps group poems across languages and civilizations.

## Examples

- love
- longing
- grief
- exile
- faith
- mortality
- silence
- hope

## Product Role

Human experiences now serve two functions:

1. discovery
2. reader preparation

An experience like longing or grief is not only a browse category.
It may also appear as a foundation that helps a reader approach a poem.
