# Symbols

Symbols are concrete images that carry layered meaning across literary traditions.

## Definition

A symbol is an image, object, place, or motif that gathers meaning through repeated use.

## Examples

- wine
- rose
- mirror
- ocean
- candle
- moth
- desert

## Important Rule

A symbol may have different meanings in different civilizations, languages, or poets.
The model should preserve those distinctions instead of flattening them into one explanation.
