# UserStories

## New Reader

- As a new reader, I want to know what background a poem assumes so I do not feel
  lost when I open it.
- As a new reader, I want short explainers for experiences like longing or mortality
  so that literary language becomes emotionally legible.
- As a new reader, I want to return to the poem after a short lesson and feel more
  prepared.

## Curious Reader

- As a curious reader, I want to click from a poem into the concepts that support it.
- As a curious reader, I want examples from other poets, films, music, and traditions
  so I can connect an idea to wider culture.
- As a curious reader, I want recommendations based on what I have already explored.

## Serious Reader

- As a serious reader, I want prerequisites framed as interpretive help rather than
  oversimplified summaries.
- As a serious reader, I want editorially grounded explanations rather than shallow
  motivational content.
- As a serious reader, I want readiness indicators that reflect familiarity, not
  artificial certification.

## Reflective Reader

- As a reflective reader, I want journal-style prompts instead of quizzes so I can
  deepen understanding through my own experience.
- As a reflective reader, I want a private map of what I have explored so I can see
  how my reading life is developing over time.

## Product-Level Story

- As a user of Maana, I want the platform to cultivate my capacity to discover meaning,
  not only explain texts to me.
