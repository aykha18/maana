# SuccessMetrics

## Reading Metrics

- poem views per active reader
- completion rate for poem pages
- click-through rate from poem to related foundations
- return-to-poem rate after opening a foundation or lesson

## Reader Formation Metrics

- lessons started
- lessons completed
- reflection prompts completed
- percentage of poem sessions that include prerequisite exploration
- number of users who move from "new to this idea" to "well prepared" for a concept

## Meaningful Outcome Metrics

- increase in poem dwell time after foundation exploration
- increase in cross-civilizational exploration after concept learning
- number of readers who follow a poem -> foundation -> poem return loop
- number of readers who build exploration depth across one experience or question

## Anti-Metrics

These should not become primary success measures:

- quiz accuracy
- streak length
- arbitrary XP totals
- inflated completion percentages detached from reading quality
